Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

General Questions

What is DigitalStore?

DigitalStore is an online marketplace for premium digital products including templates, fonts, graphics, code snippets, and courses. We connect talented creators with users looking for high-quality digital assets.

How do I purchase a product?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or checkout as a guest. After payment, you'll receive instant access to download your purchases.

Do I need to create an account to make a purchase?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track your orders, access your download history, and get updates on new products.

Products & Downloads

How do I download my purchases?

After purchase, you'll receive an email with download links. You can also access all your purchases from your account dashboard. Downloads are available instantly 24/7.

What if I lose my download link?

You can log into your account and access all your purchases from the "My Downloads" section. If you're having trouble, contact our support team for assistance.

Are there any restrictions on the number of downloads?

Each product can be downloaded up to 5 times within 30 days of purchase. If you need more downloads, please contact our support team.

What file formats do you offer?

File formats vary by product type. Common formats include: ZIP, PDF, PSD, AI, FIG, SKETCH, HTML, CSS, JS, and various font formats. Check individual product pages for specific format details.

Licensing & Usage

What license do I get with my purchase?

Most products come with a commercial license that allows you to use the product in commercial projects. Check individual product pages for specific license details. You cannot resell or redistribute the products as your own.

Can I use purchased products for client work?

Yes, with a commercial license you can use our products in client projects. However, you cannot transfer the license to your client or allow them to reuse the product for other projects.

Do I need to credit the original creator?

Crediting is appreciated but not required for most products. Some specific products may have attribution requirements - please check the license terms on the product page.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI-compliant payment processors. We never store your full credit card information on our servers.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most products if you're not satisfied. Digital products that have been downloaded may have different refund policies - check individual product details.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and bank.

Support & Technical Issues

How do I get technical support?

You can contact our support team through the Contact page. Include your order number and a detailed description of the issue for faster assistance.

What's your support response time?

We typically respond within 24 hours on business days. Urgent issues are prioritized for faster resolution.

I'm having trouble downloading a product. What should I do?

First, check your internet connection and try a different browser. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache or try using our direct download links from your account. Contact support if you continue to experience issues.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
